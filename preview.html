Direct Rubber Trade From Assam

MyRubberTradein connects rubber producers in Assam directly with factories across India, removing middlemen and ensuring transparent pricing, reliable supply chains, and better profits for both buyers and sellers.

About Us

MyRubberTradein is a direct trade platform that facilitates the supply of natural rubber from Assam to manufacturing factories across India. By eliminating intermediaries, we ensure better pricing, faster logistics, and transparent transactions between producers and industrial buyers.

What We Do

Direct Factory Supply

Rubber sourced directly from producers in Assam and delivered to factories without middlemen.

Transparent Pricing

Buyers and sellers deal directly, ensuring fair market prices and higher profit margins.

Reliable Logistics

Efficient transport coordination from Assam plantations to industrial buyers.
